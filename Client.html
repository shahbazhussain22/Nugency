Nugency is a healthcare recruitment company that recruits qualified and experienced healthcare professionals in all areas of medical, nursing, health and social care industries

Your Trusted Partner

Nugency works with independent clients to manage successful, international recruitment projects. We offer excellent service and care as standard, with tailor made recruitment packages to suit every unique requirement, large or small and at every level within the healthcare sector. It’s our passion to deliver excellence every time by helping nurses to move towards a successful career while providing our clients with the highly experienced, quality professionals and nursing staff available out there.




We strive to source the best candidates for our clients. Our experienced team works closely with each employer throughout the end-to-end recruitment process to provide quality nursing and healthcare personnel whenever needed.

What Makes Us So Special

In everything we do, we strive to make a positive difference – both for our clients and our candidates. Our service is reliable, professional, and personal – and tailored to your needs.
